Project Overview

This is a collection of mixed print and web projects, these are some of the main projects I've worked on for Creates marketing material. A lot of this material was need after Creates rebrand, this new marketing material needed to reflect the new Create brand identity throughout.

Skills & Tools

  • Photoshop
  • InDesign
  • Sketch
  • Silk Screen Printing
  • Html & Css

Print Design

Business Cards

One of the first tasks from the rebrand was to implement this design, this was the starting point for many other designs I did.

A5 Flyer Design

These flyers were to promote Create and build awareness of the free trial and have space on the flyer to have a discount sticker for new users.

Craftseller Magerzine Ad

Further Create promotion was also featured in Craftseller magazine throughout the year.

The ad space was half a page, the design wanted to be playful and have some of the craft elemtents to fit well in the magazine.

Roller Banners

These banners were to promote Create and Create Payments, these were to be used at Create events as well as meet and greets.

The boldness of the design want be direct and clear but also be a consistent design with the other print material.

Create Staff T-Shirt

To go along with the new brand Create needed T-shirts, this design had very few design elements but I wanted to keep this design minimal and use a handmade process. I went to the local print studio to get them silk screen printed, the outcome was a crisp and clean design.

Web Graphics

Web Ads

These graphics were to be used for online advertisement and Creates affiliate program. They were used in Facebook A/B tests, Craftseller newsletter email and the user sites.

The designs were to be playful, eye-catching and colourful but not overpowering. Some of the graphics had small amounts motion to them to grab the user attention.

Email Template Design

The two main tasks was update the previous email template to be responsive and visual closer to the new brand direction.

The design to the right was the final design of many iterations then new email wanted to have a clear call to action, a human element which was the photo of the customer account manager (not the nice kitten) and have a warm welcome with the use of colour and imagery.

Halloween Email Design

This was a playful and creative project for a discount email, this went out to Create users and potentially new customers over the halloween holiday.

MailChimp Email Template

This was one of many emails I put together, after designing the email banner graphics I would also build a template to keep the email newsletters consistent.
