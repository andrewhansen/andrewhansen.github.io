Project Overview

This is a collection of mixed print and web projects, these are some of the main projects I've worked on for Create's marketing material. A lot of this material was needed after Create's rebrand, this new marketing material needed to reflect the new Create brand identity throughout.

Skills & Tools

  • Photoshop
  • InDesign
  • Sketch
  • Silk Screen Printing
  • Html & Css

Print Design

Business Cards

One of the first tasks from the rebrand was to implement this design, this was the starting point for many other designs I did.

A5 Flyer Design

These flyers were to promote Create and build awareness of the free trial. The flyers needed to consider space for any potential discount sticker for new users.

Craftseller Magazine Ad

Further Create promotion was also featured in Craftseller magazine throughout the year.

The ad space was half a page and needed to display Create's playful style. It was aimed at the craft audience and so was visually inline with this industry.

Roller Banners

The above banners were create to promote Create and its new product. They were used at Create events, exhibitions as well as meet and greets.

I created the design to be bold, direct and clear. I also took into consideration the brand consistency and any other print material.

Create Staff T-Shirt

In order to build company awareness and following the redesign, I created staff t-shirts. The design was minimal using a handmade process, nodding to the Create customer. I went to the local print studio to get them silk screen printed, the outcome was a crisp and clean.

Web Graphics

Web Ads

I created these graphics for online advertisement and Create's affiliate program. They were used in Facebook A/B tests, the Craftseller newsletter email and on user affiliate sites.

The designs were to be playful, eye-catching and colourful. Some of the graphics had motion in order to grab the user attention.

Email Template Design

The two main tasks were to update the previous non-responsive email, to responsive, and to evolve along with the rest of the brand's design.

The design on right was the end result. The new design included a clear call to action, a photo of the customer account manager for a more human element (not the nice kitten) and give a sense of a warm welcome through colour and imagery.

Halloween Email Design

This playful and creative project was for a discount email campaign. It was sent to all current Create users and potential new customers over the Halloween holiday.

MailChimp Email Template

This was one of many email templates I put together. After designing the email banner graphics I would also build a template to keep the email consistent for future newsletters.
